### 🔬 Research Focus
I am a researcher dedicated to understanding the complex dynamics of **zoonotic bacteria**, with a specialized focus on **Streptococcus species**. My work bridges the gap between traditional microbiology and computational genomics.

- **Genomic Diversity**: Investigating the evolutionary trajectories and population structures of bacterial pathogens.
- **Virulence Mechanisms**: Identifying and characterizing factors that drive pathogenicity and host-adaptation.
- **Regulatory Networks**: Deciphering how bacterial systems sense and respond to environmental cues.
- **AMR Surveillance**: Monitoring the distribution and transmission dynamics of **Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)** genes.

- **Dry Lab:** Python (Biopython, Pandas), R (Tidyverse, Phylogenetics), Shell Scripting, Linux Environment.
- **Bioinformatics:** Genome Assembly (Unicycler, Flye), Annotation (Prokka), Comparative Genomics (Roary, FastTree), AMR Profiling (ResFinder, CARD).
- **Wet Lab:** Microbial Culturing, DNA/RNA Extraction, NGS Library Preparation, PCR/qPCR.
